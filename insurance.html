FAQS

Q?

Why should I buy through Ruturaj?

A.

Ruturaj is a household name in the used car industry. With a presence in 70+ cities across the country, it’s a one-stop solution for all your car transactional needs- Sale, Purchase, and Finance. Wide range of pre-owned cars at one place Trained Professionals to help you buy your car with comprehensive quality checks Buy pre-owned cars only from verified customers- no middlemen Hassle-Free RC Transfer Easy financing options- On spot loan approval Rahulraj MoneyBack Guarantee- 14 days return assurance (T&C apply)

Q?

How do I get in touch with Ruturaj if I am interested in buying a car?

A.

You can simply share your details on our website against the car you are interested in buying. Our representative will connect with you, accompany you or meet you at the car location and help you buy the car of your choice.

Q?

What is the business model of Ruturaj?

A.

We at Ruturaj believe in changing the way people sell their cars in India by simplifying and standardizing the entire process. We buy your car and sell it to one of our 10,000+ channel partners across various cities in India. We charge a nominal fee on every transaction when you sell your car to us. The service charge details can be seen in the Transaction Guidelines section.

Q?

Where do I need to come to test drive the car?

A.

The relationship manager assigned to you would coordinate for your meetings with the verified seller at the seller’s location. Some cars are ready to be bought in a single visit and you can come to our Car Hubs for the test drive.

Q?

Do I need to pay for the test drive?

A.

You need not pay anything for the test drive. Once you find the right car, you simply have to pay a token amount to finalize the deal.

Q?

Does Ruturaj provide any warranty on the cars?

A.

Ruturaj offers different warranty packages on select cars. Buyers have an option to buy warranty powered by Ruturaj depending on their requirements such as cost coverage, components, etc. The packages are applicable based on the Ruturaj Inspection Report.

Q?

How will the ownership of the car be transferred?

A.

If you buy a car from Ruturaj, you can avail our hassle-free RC Transfer service at a nominal charge. Alternatively, you can choose to get the RC transferred at the RTO yourself, for which we hold back a certain amount till it is completed.
